# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

obdex is a community project for sharing automotive diagnostic knowledge. We expect everyone interacting with the project — issues, pull requests, discussions — to follow these basic rules:

- **Be respectful.** Disagreement is fine; personal attacks are not.
- **Stay technical.** Discussions should be about codes, sources, evidence, schema, tooling. Off-topic political or commercial content does not belong here.
- **Cite, don't assert.** Claims about a code's meaning, cause, or repair need a source. Workshop folklore alone is not enough.
- **Assume good faith.** Most disagreements are misunderstandings, not malice.

## Unacceptable Behaviour

- Harassment, discriminatory language, or personal attacks.
- Pasting verbatim copyrighted text from manuals, paid databases, or service literature.
- Spam, self-promotion unrelated to OBD-II diagnostics.
- Repeatedly opening issues without sources after being asked.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, close, or delete content that violates this policy and may block users who repeatedly do so. Report problems to **z@xdr5.de**.
